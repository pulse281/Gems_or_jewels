About the App

Dreamland Story is a delightful and innovative match-3 puzzle game set in a whimsical world of dreams. Players embark on a journey through various dreamscapes, solving puzzles to help Mia and her friends defeat the Sandman. The game offers a unique take on the match-3 genre, combining traditional elements with fresh mechanics and beautiful dream-themed visuals. With hundreds of levels, daily challenges, and engaging storylines, Dreamland Story is perfect for players looking to enjoy a creative and captivating puzzle experience.
